Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Data We Collect: We collect your name, email, shipping address, and order history when you make a purchase or create an account. We do not store payment card details — all payments are processed by Stripe or Razorpay.

How We Use It: Your data is used solely to process orders, send shipping updates, and improve your experience. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Cookies: We use essential cookies for session management and optional analytics cookies. You can decline analytics cookies via the cookie banner.

Your Rights: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at privacy@modart.com.

Contact: For privacy concerns, email privacy@modart.com.

Terms & Conditions

Last updated: January 2025

Products: All ModArt garments are made-to-order. Once production begins, orders cannot be cancelled. Sizes and colors are as described — minor variations in print placement (±5mm) are within acceptable tolerance.

Creator Drops: Creators retain full rights to their uploaded artwork. By listing a design on ModArt, you grant us a non-exclusive licence to reproduce it on garments sold through the platform. Creator commission is 15% of net revenue per unit sold.

Returns: Unworn items may be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Custom-designed pieces are non-refundable unless defective. Shipping costs for returns are the customer's responsibility.

Governing Law: These terms are governed by the laws of India. Disputes shall be resolved in the courts of Mumbai, Maharashtra.
